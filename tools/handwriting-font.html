How to use

1

Write your characters

Write each letter clearly on paper — uppercase (A-Z), lowercase (a-z), and numbers (0-9). Keep good spacing between characters. Use a dark pen on white paper for best results.

2

Upload your photo

Take a clear, well-lit photo of your handwriting and upload it. You can drag and drop the image or click to browse. Make sure the image is not blurry and has good contrast.

3

Map each character

Draw a box around each character on the image. The current character is shown in the top-left badge. Click and drag to draw a rectangle around the letter. Smart Fit is enabled by default — it automatically snaps the box tightly around the character.

4

Adjust boxes

You can move any box by clicking and dragging inside it. Resize by dragging the corner or edge handles. Click a character in the sidebar to jump to it. Use Skip to skip a character and Undo to remove the last box.

5

Generate & Download

Click Generate to create your font. You can preview it by typing text, then download as .otf or .woff. Install the .otf file on your computer to use it in any app.

Tips for best results

• Write on unlined white paper with a thick, dark pen
• Keep letters roughly the same size
• Leave space between characters so they don't touch
• Take the photo straight-on (not at an angle)
• Good lighting = better character detection
• You can edit the character list to add symbols like ! @ # etc.
